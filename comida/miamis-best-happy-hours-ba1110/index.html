Miami’s Best Happy Hours

What's happening

Miami's best happy hour spots are offering time-sensitive deals on drinks, oysters, and more across the city's bar scene. From cocktails under $10 to skyline views, venues ranging from longtime local institutions to Michelin-starred newcomers are competing for happy hour crowds.

Who's involved

Eater Miami and various Miami establishments including Monty's, rooftop bars, and Michelin-starred restaurants.

Why it matters

Happy hour deals are a way for Miami's diverse restaurant and bar scene to attract post-work crowds and drive business during slower hours. The variety of options across different venue types gives locals and visitors affordable access to quality drinks and dining experiences.
