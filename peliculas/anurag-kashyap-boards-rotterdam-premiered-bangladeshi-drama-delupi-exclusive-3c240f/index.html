Anurag Kashyap Boards Rotterdam-Premiered Bangladeshi Drama ‘Delupi’ (EXCLUSIVE)

What's happening

Indian filmmaker Anurag Kashyap has joined Bangladeshi drama 'Delupi' as its presenter, lending his name and brand to the film's release and marketing efforts. The film, which premiered at Rotterdam Film Festival, will now carry the 'Anurag Kashyap Presents' banner.

Who's involved

Anurag Kashyap is a prominent Indian auteur known for directing critically acclaimed films like 'Gangs of Wasseypur' and 'Udta Punjab.' 'Delupi' is a Bangladeshi drama that debuted at the prestigious Rotterdam International Film Festival's Bright Future section.

Why it matters

Kashyap's involvement significantly boosts the film's visibility and credibility in Indian and international markets, potentially opening doors for wider theatrical and streaming distribution. This cross-border collaboration between Indian and Bangladeshi cinema strengthens regional film industry ties and expands opportunities for South Asian filmmakers.

More context

Kashyap's 'Presents' banner has become a mark of quality in Indian independent cinema, having championed various emerging filmmakers and stories. The Rotterdam Film Festival's Bright Future section focuses on innovative work by international filmmakers, making 'Delupi's' selection there a notable achievement. This move reflects a growing trend of established filmmakers using their platforms to amplify voices from neighboring film industries, particularly as streaming platforms increase demand for diverse international content.
