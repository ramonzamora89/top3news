Greek Myth Summer Continues as Mediawan Rolls Out Kinetic Content Gameshow ‘Trojan Horse’ Across Europe

What's happening

French production company Mediawan is launching a new unscripted gameshow format called "Trojan Horse" across Europe this summer. The high-concept format, created by Kinetic Content, capitalizes on the current surge of Greek mythology content in entertainment.

Who's involved

Mediawan, a major French media producer, is partnering with Kinetic Content, a production company known for developing innovative unscripted formats. The rollout coincides with Christopher Nolan's upcoming film adaptation of Homer's "The Odyssey."

Why it matters

The format taps into renewed mainstream interest in Greek mythology, potentially capturing audiences already engaged with classical narratives. Success across multiple European markets could establish "Trojan Horse" as a franchise property and validate mythology-themed entertainment as a durable content trend beyond film.

More context

Greek mythology has become a major entertainment focus, with major filmmakers like Christopher Nolan adapting classical epics for theatrical release. Mediawan and Kinetic Content have track records developing formats that travel across international markets. Unscripted gameshows with high-concept themes have demonstrated strong viewership potential in European markets. This summer launch positions the format to benefit from the cultural momentum building around mythological storytelling across multiple media platforms.
