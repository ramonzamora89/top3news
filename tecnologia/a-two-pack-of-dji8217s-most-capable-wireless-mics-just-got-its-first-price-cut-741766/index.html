A two-pack of DJI’s most capable wireless mics just got its first price cut

What's happening

DJI's Mic 3 wireless microphone kit is receiving its first price reduction. The two-pack system, which includes a charging case, is now more affordable for content creators looking to improve mobile video audio.

Who's involved

DJI is a Chinese technology company that dominates the consumer drone market and has expanded into audio and imaging accessories. The Mic 3 represents their latest entry into the wireless microphone space for smartphone videographers.

Why it matters

Smartphone microphones remain a significant weak point in mobile video production despite cameras becoming increasingly sophisticated. This price cut makes professional-grade wireless audio more accessible to creators, streamers, and journalists who rely on smartphones for video work.

More context

Smartphone video quality has advanced dramatically with computational photography and stabilization, but built-in microphones still struggle with wind noise and distance recording. The wireless lavalier microphone category has grown as content creation on platforms like TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram accelerates. DJI's entry into this market leverages their reputation for consumer tech innovation, though they face competition from established audio brands. Price cuts on new tech products typically indicate either strong sales momentum or inventory management, and signal the product line's transition into mainstream adoption.
